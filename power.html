Divine

Focus

Driven by swift reflexes and quick thinking, thieves and archers deal damage in short, quick bursts before vanishing in the shadows. Focus, unlike most power sources, is used up very quickly, but also regenerates very quickly.

Primal

Drawing power directly from primal instincts causes barbarians and druids to become more powerful as the battle grows longer. Primal-powered classes are focused on resource generation, as the taste of blood causes them to be able to continue, stronger, in battle.

Special case: Ethereal

Ethereal have no power source; instead, each Ethereal ability has its own independent cooldown. The focus of an Ethereal is not to conserve any resource, but to use the right cooldowns at the right times.
