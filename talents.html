Explaination

  • You have an amount of talent points equal to your current level.
  • The first talent point may be spent on any basic-level talent.
  • After the first talent is chosen, talents from the basic level of that class may be chosen, but not other classes.
  • At level 200, the talent trees of the classes directly before and directly after the chosen class may be used as well.
  • Every 15 levels, one new talent level, containing three talents, is unlocked.
  • In order to add a point to a talent outside of the basic level, the talent directly before the one recieving the point – that is, the talent in the same position in the previous level – must have at least the new amount of points, OR the talent level directly before the talent must contain double the number of points, in the same class, as the talent will contain after adding the point.

Basic

Cleric

Holy waters
Increases mana regeneration greatly outside of combat and slightly while in combat.
Cleansing waters
Your spells have a chance to shift debuffs from allies to enemies.
Leeching waters
Spell: Applies a debuff in an area that slowly converts enemy health into mana for the cleric.

Paladin

Plague ward
Reduces the power of damage over time effects cast by enemies that are focusing their attacks on you.
Light of dawn
Increases the effect of healing spells on the paladin.
Retribution
Enemies dealing damage to allies are, themselves, dealt damage.

Barbarian

Sharpened blade
Increases critical strike chance.
Heavy blows
Increases weapon damage.
Impenetrable blockade
Decreases damage taken from melee attacks.

Monk

Thief

Shadow lurker
When stealthed, your movement speed is increased.
Shadow ambusher
Your attacks that cause you to lose stealth deal increased damage.
Deadly poisons
Increases damage dealt by your poison effects.

Archer

Poisoned arrows
Enemies you have recently damaged are dealt extra damage over time.
Swiftness of the fox
When you are dealt damage, you regain focus.
Giant quiver
Increases your maximum focus.

Druid

The bear
Increases damage soaking while shapeshifted.
The cat
Increases damage dealt while shapeshifted.
The scorpion
Deals extra damage over time on enemies you have recently damaged while shapeshifted.

Mystic

Necromancer

Plaguebringer
Spell: Deal one tick of damage from each of your diseases to each enemy other than the one afflicted by the disease. Passive: Your single-target spells deal damage over time instead of immediately.
Demonmaster
Your damaging spells have a chance to open a rift to the netherworld, bringing demons to your aid.

Ethereal

Time warp
Enemies that deal damage to the ethereal find that their attacks and movement become slower.
